Basic Game Workings

1- Get all commands from all players
2- Move all pieces according to commands
3- Resolve conflicts, move pieces as necessary, add captures to reserve
4- Repeat step 3 as necessary (BOUNCE)
5- Resolve any flag captures, add captures to reserves (email losers)
6- Begin new turn (email remaining players for next turn)

Other basic workings
- Delete games after 30-45 days of inactivity
